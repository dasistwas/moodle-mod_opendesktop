Open Virtual Desktop - Desktopsize

This feature works only in Firefox. For Internet Explorer adjust the size of your browser-window manually and then start the OpenDesktop.

If you want to have a full-screen display, click on F11 to maximize the browser window and then start your OpenDesktop

This option allows you to choose the size of your OpenDesktop window. If you work with someone with a smaller screen than yours and you choose the whole window-size, your partner will only see a scaled down window of the OpenDesktop.

Therefore we recommend to check if the size of the OpenDesktop window fits in the computer-screen of the persons watching your OpenDesktop. (Choosing the window-size of the participant with the smallest screen would be a good choice.)
